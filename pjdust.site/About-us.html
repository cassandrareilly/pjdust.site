About Us

Since 1980, we have have grown into the UKâ€™s most reputable and innovative specialist Local Exhaust Ventilation (LEV) engineers, providing bespoke extraction systems and delivering thousands of LEV tests each year throughout the UK.

 Here at P&J Dust, we are specialists at controlling airborne contaminants. Each year British workers contract occupational asthma and other lung diseases due to breathing in too much dust and fumes. This is due to workplaces not having the correct control measures in place. This is where we come in.


Fully trained with a wealth of health and safety knowledge for you, we have an extensive list of accreditations (see full list) and memberships with national and global associations (such as: British Safety Council, Institute of Occupational Safety & Health and American Conference Government Industrial Hygienists) to keep up to date.

We excel at innovative design of extraction systems. All of our units are built by us, for you and they are made to measure. See our design process.

We are based in Sheerness, Kent where we manufacture all of the units that you find throughout the UK. See our manufacturing process.

But we do not stop there. After installation, we provide all companies with annual LEV tests to ensure everything keeps working as it did when we first made it.

Through the combination of maintenance, repairs, LEV testing and personal sampling our dedicated team are able to help your workforce stay safe while your business continues to grow.
